Certification FAQ

How long does my certificate stay valid?
Most certs remain valid for 2–3 years. Some (like CPR) require annual refreshers. Your certificate shows the exact expiry.
Do I get a physical card?
Yes—digital certificates are instant. You can also request an optional printed wallet card during checkout.
How can employers verify?
Each certificate has a unique ID (e.g., CERT-OS10-001). Employers can enter it on our Verify page to confirm authenticity.
